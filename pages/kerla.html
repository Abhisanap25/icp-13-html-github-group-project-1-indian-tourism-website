About the kerla:

Kerala is a state located on the southwestern coast of India, along the Arabian Sea. It shares borders with Karnataka in the north and Tamil Nadu in the east.Kerala has a rich history
dating back over 3,000 years.It was an important spice trade hub, attracting traders from Greece, Rome, Arabia, China, and Europe.Colonized by the Portuguese, Dutch, and British,
you can still see their influence in cities like Kochi.It was formed on November 1, 1956, by merging Malayalam-speaking regions.Western Ghats on the east and Arabian Sea on the west.

  • Backwaters: A network of lagoons and canals (e.g., Alleppey)
  • Beaches: Kovalam, Varkala
  • Hill Stations: Munnar, Wayanad
  • Wildlife Sanctuaries: Periyar, Silent Valley,

Culture and People:

Kerala is known for its rich cultural heritage, classical arts like Kathakali and Mohiniyattam, and religious harmony. The main language spoken is Malayalam. Important festivals include
Onam and Vishu.Kerala has the highest literacy rate in India (over 96%) and excellent education and healthcare systems.

  • Kathakali: Classical dance-drama with elaborate makeup and colorful costumes.
  • Mohiniyattam: Graceful solo female classical dance.
  • Theyyam: Ritualistic folk dance performed in temples and villages.
  • Ottamthullal and Koodiyattam: Other unique traditional performances.
